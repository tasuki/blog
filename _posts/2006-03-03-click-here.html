"To visit my wonderful art/test page click here."

How annoying do you find that? I really hate it, it isn't good for the reader, who is skimming the text (yes, people on the web actually don't read, they just skim web pages) and would like to know where that wonderful link points. It isn't good for search engines as the keywords "click here" are not connected to the content of the linked page, and it is just plain annoying.

"You might also want to check my wonderful art/test page."

This is much better, the clicking user knows what he is clicking and search engines are happy as well.

I am sorry if you find it natural, I find it natural too, but obviously many people don't. So I felt urgent need to blog about it (that is what blog is for anyway, right? :-)).
