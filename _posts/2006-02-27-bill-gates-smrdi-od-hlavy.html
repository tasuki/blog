Sorry for the czech title of this post, but I just couldn't resist (if you don't understand it don't be sad, I'll explain it later).

I went to the town center today to pay something in a bank, to get a haircut (after half a year I think :-)) and to take some photos, as the sun was shining nicely and in quite the right angle.

This ad cought my attention because of the dinosaurs' heads. When I read the text it almost killed me on the spot, it read: "Bill Gates smrdí od hlavy", which means something like "Bill Gates' head smells". The footnote states something about Microsoft and evolution, whatever.

I especilly appreciate how precisely it was done, the bubble with text is exactly where the original one was. For some reason I find the text incredibly funny (maybe it is because Microsoft paid a lot of money (yup, the money they sucked from you poor Microsoft customers) for the ad and it actually turns against them).
