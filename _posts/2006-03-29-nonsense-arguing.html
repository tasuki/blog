Me: "2+3=5"
Stupid: "No, that is not true, 1+1=2"
Others: "Hmmm, Stupid is correct, 1+1=2 ... tasuki, you should accept that!"
Me: "Well, duh, of course 1+1=2, but that has nothing to do with 2+3=5"
Others: "tasuki should really calm down as it is completely clear that we are correct and 1+1=2"
Stupid: "See? I said it from the beginning that 1+1=2"
*tasuki gives up*

I really don't know why so many people use arguments that are true, but completely irrelevant to the matter. I already met dozens of people in my life who usually argue this way. I find it really annoying and I am perplexed that they can be sooo utterly stupid. Guess I should follow the saying: "Never argue with idiots. They drag you down to their level and then beat you with years of experience".
