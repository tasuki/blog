There are many ways to write smileys: some people do not write closing parenthesis after smiley (as it is the smiley itself and the closing parenthesis might seem as if it was more smiling smiley) and some do.

Some people write punctation before smileys, some write it after smileys and some do not write the punctation near smileys at all (as it seems as a birth mark of the smiley).

The only thing I do not like is people writing smileys without space before them, sticked to the word in front of them. That drives me mad.
