It's crazy, I recieve aproximately 10 spam comments every hour. :x It used to be like one or two spam comments a day, but not anymore...

So of course I had to take some countermeasures, the first one is: the author must have a previously approved comment for the new one to appear instantly. If it isn't enough, I will continue to make it harder for spammers... you will have to have an approved account, for example. But I'd really hate to do that as it would stop most of already rare genuine comments. :(
