Colors are often neglected or people even choose as shiny colors as possible. It hurts eyes and makes any text unreadable. All people working with colors should at least read color theory overwiew. I wonder why they never taught us this in school.

If you are seeking good color combinations then visit color combos, with hundreds of color combinations prepared for you to use. A lot of them are rather crappy but if you look around a bit you can find some really wonderful ones.

[Edit 2018] There are now nicer tools for choosing colors. They also help with color combinations!
